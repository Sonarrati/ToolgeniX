Frequently Asked Questions

How many images can I convert to PDF at once?

You can convert as many images as you want, but keep in mind that more images will result in larger file sizes and longer processing times. For optimal performance, we recommend converting up to 50 images at a time.

What's the maximum image size I can use?

There's no strict limit, but very large images may take longer to process. For best results, use images under 10MB each.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, and GIF formats for input. The output is always a PDF file.

Can I reorder the images in my PDF?

Yes! You can drag and drop the image previews to rearrange them in any order before generating the PDF.

Why is my PDF file size large?

PDF file size depends on the number of images, their dimensions, and quality. To reduce file size, use fewer images or compress your images before conversion.
