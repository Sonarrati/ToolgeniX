How to Use Our Unit Converter

  1. Select a category from the dropdown menu (length, mass, temperature, etc.).
  2. Choose the "From" unit - the unit you want to convert from.
  3. Choose the "To" unit - the unit you want to convert to.
  4. Enter the value you want to convert in the input field.
  5. Click "Convert" or press Enter to see the converted result.
  6. Adjust precision using the dropdown to control decimal places in the result.
  7. Save conversions to your history for future reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the unit conversions?

Our unit converter uses precise conversion factors and follows international standards for all calculations. The results are accurate to the decimal precision you specify.

What units and categories are supported?

We support 9 categories: Length, Mass, Temperature, Area, Volume, Speed, Time, Data, and Pressure with multiple units in each category.

Can I save my conversion history?

Yes, the converter automatically saves your conversion history in your browser's local storage. You can export it as CSV or clear it at any time.

Does the tool work offline?

Yes, since all calculations happen locally in your browser, the tool works completely offline once the page is loaded.

Can I use voice input for values?

Yes, the tool supports voice input for entering values if your browser supports speech recognition.

Is there a limit to the number of conversions I can do?

No, there's no limit. You can perform as many conversions as you need, and the history stores up to 120 recent conversions.
