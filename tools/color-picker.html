Frequently Asked Questions

What color formats does the converter support?

Our color converter supports HEX, RGB, and HSL formats with instant conversion between all formats. All conversions happen in real-time as you change values.

Can I extract colors from images?

Yes, you can upload any image and click on it to pick colors, or use the automatic palette extraction feature to get dominant colors from the image.

Does the tool check color contrast for accessibility?

Yes, the tool includes WCAG contrast checking with multiple standards (AA normal text, AA large text, AAA) to ensure your color combinations are accessible to all users.

How many colors can I save as favorites?

You can save an unlimited number of colors as favorites. They are stored locally in your browser for quick access across sessions.

Is my image data stored or uploaded to servers?

No, all image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I export my color palettes?

Yes, you can export palettes as JSON files, download them as PNG images, or copy CSS gradients for immediate use in your projects.
