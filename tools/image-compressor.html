How to Use Our Image Compressor

  1. Upload your image by clicking the "Upload Your Image" area or dragging and dropping your file. We support JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP formats.
  2. Adjust compression quality using the slider. Higher quality means larger file size but better image quality. Lower quality reduces file size more but may affect image appearance.
  3. Click the "Compress Image" button to process your image. The compression happens instantly in your browser.
  4. Compare the results using the side-by-side preview and check the file size savings.
  5. Download your compressed image by clicking the "Download Compressed Image" button.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will compression reduce my image quality?

Some quality reduction is inevitable with compression, but our tool allows you to control the balance between file size and quality. At higher quality settings (80%+), the difference is often barely noticeable.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend images under 20MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP formats for both input and output.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Currently, our compressor processes one image at a time. For batch compression, you'll need to compress each image separately.

What's the best compression quality setting?

For most purposes, we recommend starting with 80% quality. This typically reduces file size significantly while maintaining good visual quality. Adjust based on your specific needs.
