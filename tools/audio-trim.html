How to Use Our Audio Trimmer

  1. Upload your audio file by clicking the "Upload Your Audio File" area or dragging and dropping your file. We support MP3, WAV, AAC, OGG, and FLAC formats.
  2. Set the start and end times for trimming. Use the format HH:MM:SS (Hours:Minutes:Seconds).
  3. Click the "Trim Audio" button to process your audio. The trimming happens in your browser.
  4. Wait for processing - this may take a few moments depending on file size and complexity.
  5. Preview the trimmed audio using the audio player.
  6. Download your trimmed audio by clicking the "Download Trimmed Audio" button.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does audio trimming take?

Trimming time depends on file length and your device's processing power. Most files are processed in a few seconds to minutes.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend files under 100MB for optimal performance. Larger files may be slow to process or fail on devices with limited memory.

Are my audio files secure?

Absolutely! All audio processing happens directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What audio formats do you support?

We support MP3, WAV, AAC, OGG, and FLAC formats for both input and output.

Can I trim multiple files at once?

Currently, our trimmer processes one audio file at a time. For multiple files, you'll need to trim each one separately.

What's the best format for trimmed audio?

MP3 is generally the best choice for most uses as it offers good quality with reasonable file size and wide compatibility across devices.
