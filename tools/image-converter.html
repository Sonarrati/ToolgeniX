Supported Image Formats

PNG

Lossless compression format ideal for graphics with transparency. Best for logos, icons, and images requiring high quality.

JPEG

Lossy compression format perfect for photographs. Offers good quality with smaller file sizes.

WebP

Modern format by Google offering both lossy and lossless compression. Better compression than PNG and JPEG.

GIF

Supports animation and transparency. Limited to 256 colors, making it ideal for simple graphics.

BMP

Uncompressed format that preserves maximum quality but results in large file sizes.

TIFF

High-quality format often used in professional photography and printing industries.

PDF

Convert your image to a PDF document, useful for sharing or printing multiple images as a single file.

Yes, our image converter is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or usage limits.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

Our converter can handle most standard image sizes. For optimal performance, we recommend images under 10MB.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Currently, our converter processes one image at a time. For batch conversion, you'll need to convert each image separately.

Do you support RAW camera formats?

No, our converter focuses on standard image formats. For RAW camera files, you'll need specialized software.
