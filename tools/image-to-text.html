How to Use Our Image to Text OCR Tool

  1. Upload your image by clicking the "Upload Your Image" area or dragging and dropping your file. We support JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP formats.
  2. Click the "Extract Text" button to process your image. Our OCR technology will analyze the image and extract any text it finds.
  3. Wait for processing - this usually takes a few seconds depending on image complexity and text amount.
  4. Review the extracted text displayed in the text area. You can edit the text if needed.
  5. Copy the text by clicking the "Copy Text" button to use it in other applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the text extraction?

The accuracy depends on image quality, text clarity, and font type. Clear, high-contrast images with standard fonts typically yield the best results.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend images under 10MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP formats for input.

Can I extract text from handwritten notes?

Our OCR works best with printed text. Handwritten text recognition is less accurate and may require clearer handwriting for good results.

What languages does the OCR support?

Our tool currently supports English text extraction. Support for additional languages is planned for future updates.
