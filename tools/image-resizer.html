How to Use Our Image Resizer

  1. Upload your image by clicking the "Upload Your Image" area or dragging and dropping your file. We support JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, and GIF formats.
  2. Set your desired dimensions by entering width and height in pixels, or use the percentage option to scale proportionally.
  3. Check "Maintain aspect ratio" if you want to preserve the original proportions of your image.
  4. Click the "Resize Image" button to process your image. The resizing happens in your browser.
  5. Compare the original and resized images using the side-by-side preview.
  6. Download your resized image by clicking the "Download Resized Image" button.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does maintaining aspect ratio work?

When you check "Maintain aspect ratio" and enter only one dimension (width or height), the other dimension is automatically calculated to preserve the original proportions of your image.

Will resizing affect image quality?

Reducing image size generally maintains good quality, but enlarging images may result in some quality loss as pixels are interpolated.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, and GIF formats for both input and output.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Currently, our resizer processes one image at a time. For multiple images, you'll need to resize each one separately.

What's the maximum image size I can resize?

There's no strict limit, but extremely large images may be slow to process or fail on devices with limited memory.
