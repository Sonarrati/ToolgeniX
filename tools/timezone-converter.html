How to Use Our Timezone Converter

  1. Select a date and time using the datetime picker or click "Now" to use the current time.
  2. Choose your "From" timezone from the dropdown list (your local timezone is automatically detected).
  3. Select your "To" timezone from the dropdown list (the timezone you want to convert to).
  4. Click "Convert" to see the converted time in your target timezone.
  5. Use the "Swap" button to quickly swap between the "From" and "To" timezones.
  6. Copy the result or add it to your calendar for easy reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the timezone conversion?

Our timezone converter uses the Luxon library with Intl API support, ensuring highly accurate conversions that automatically handle Daylight Saving Time (DST) changes and timezone offsets.

Does the converter handle Daylight Saving Time?

Yes, the converter automatically accounts for Daylight Saving Time changes based on the date and location, providing accurate conversions throughout the year.

How many timezones are supported?

The converter supports all standard IANA timezone identifiers, including all major global timezones like UTC, EST, PST, CET, IST, and many more.

Can I add converted times to my calendar?

Yes, you can add converted times directly to your calendar by clicking the "Add to Calendar" button, which generates an ICS file that can be imported into most calendar applications.

Is my location data stored?

No, we only detect your local timezone for convenience, but this information is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Does the tool work on mobile devices?

Yes, our timezone converter is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
