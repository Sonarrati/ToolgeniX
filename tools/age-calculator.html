Frequently Asked Questions

How long does conversion take?

Conversion time depends on video length, resolution, and your device's processing power. Short videos typically take a few seconds to minutes, while longer videos may take longer.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend videos under 50MB for optimal performance. Larger files may be slow to process or fail on devices with limited memory.

Are my videos secure?

Absolutely! All video processing happens directly in your browser. Your videos are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What video formats do you support?

We support MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM, and other popular video formats for both input and output.

Can I convert multiple videos at once?

Currently, our converter processes one video at a time. For batch conversion, you'll need to convert each video separately.

What's the best format for general use?

MP4 is generally the best choice for most uses as it offers good quality with reasonable file size and wide compatibility across devices and platforms.
