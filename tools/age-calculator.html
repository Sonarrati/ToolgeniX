How to Use Our Age Calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth in the date field. This is required to calculate your age.
  2. Optionally enter your time of birth for more precise calculations including hours and minutes.
  3. Click the "Calculate Age" button to see your exact age and countdown to your next birthday.
  4. Review the results which show your age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes.
  5. Check the countdown to see how many days are left until your next birthday celebration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the age calculation?

Our age calculator is extremely accurate, calculating your age down to the minute if you provide your time of birth. Without the time, it calculates based on the start of the day.

Do I need to enter my time of birth?

No, the time of birth is optional. If you don't enter it, the calculation will assume your birth time was 00:00 (midnight).

Can I calculate age for future dates?

No, the calculator only works for dates in the past. Future dates will show an error message.

Is my birth date stored anywhere?

No, all calculations happen locally in your browser. Your birth date is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

How does the birthday countdown work?

The countdown calculates the number of days from today until your next birthday. If your birthday has already passed this year, it counts to your birthday next year.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Yes, you can use this calculator to determine anyone's age by entering their date of birth.
